# Report and Demo Spec

## Objective
Prepare the deliverable templates needed for interim and final submission.

## Required Outputs

### Interim
- reconnaissance content
- architecture diagram section
- progress summary
- early accuracy observations
- known gaps and plan

### Final
- comparison of manual vs generated Day-One answers
- finalized architecture diagram
- accuracy analysis
- limitations
- FDE applicability
- self-audit results
- 6-minute demo checklist

## Required Supporting Templates
- `RECONNAISSANCE.md`
- interim report outline
- final report outline
- demo walkthrough checklist

## Acceptance Criteria
- templates map directly to produced artifacts
- demo checklist matches actual system behavior
- report structure is immediately usable
